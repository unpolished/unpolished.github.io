Connect & Grow

Unpolished is open to any aspiring entrepreneur, regardless of your beliefs, because we want to grow our community together.
Questions?

Questions?

We've got answers!

  • Do I have to be a Christian?
    Nope

    Unpolished is rooted in the Christian faith but you do not have to be Christian to get involved. Our events and programming will always be based in Christianity, but our doors are open for you!

  • How can I get involved?
    Show up!

    What we love about entrepreneurs is their ability to get things accomplished quickly and efficiently. Do you want to volunteer? Is there a subgroup you think is needed within the Unpolished community. Shoot us an email!

  • Who can I talk to about my business?
    Everyone!

    Get active in the Unpolished community. Attend events, volunteer or connect with the Unpolished community. There are so many ways to get involved!

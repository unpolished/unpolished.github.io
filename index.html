Connect & Grow

Unpolished is open to any aspiring entrepreneur, regardless of your beliefs, because we want to grow our community together.
Questions?

Questions?

We've got answers!

  • What is the relationship between Unpolished and Crossroads?
    Awesome, that's what!

    Unpolished is a concept born out of a group of like minded, entrepreneurial and faith based individuals who are active members and attendees within Crossroads. However, we are not strictly associated with Crossroads. We operate as a separate entity with the goal of connecting with the greater entrepreneurial community at large.

  • Are your events for Crossroads attendees only?
    Nope, we welcome everybody!

    Come one entrepreneur come all! From our speaker series to our co-working days, no Unpolished event is for Crossroads attendees only. We welcome anyone and everyone who wish to engage in our events. We want to help our city’s growing entrepreneurial culture as a whole.

  • Do I have to be a Christian to be involved?
    Nope

    Unpolished is rooted in the Christian faith but you do not have to be Christian to get involved. Our events and programming will always be based in Christianity, but our doors are open for you!

  • How can I get involved?
    Show up!

    What we love about entrepreneurs is their ability to get things accomplished quickly and efficiently. Do you want to volunteer? Is there a subgroup you think is needed within the Unpolished community. Shoot us an email!

  • Who can I talk to about my business?
    Everyone!

    Get active in the Unpolished community. Attend events, volunteer or connect with the Unpolished community. There are so many ways to get involved!

  • Why do you serve beer?
    Mmm, beer.

    Beer is delicious, but that neither here nor there. We want unpolished to feel different from your standard “church events.” It’s not really about the beer, it’s about community, networking, building relationships and starting a conversation. A nice beer can help act as an ice-breaker. We expect everyone to drink responsibly.

  • What is the leadership structure of Unpolished?
    Highly fractal...

    Just kidding. The leadership structure of Unpolished is comprised of a group of 10 men and women who are regular Crossroads attendees. The team is responsible for programming and events.
